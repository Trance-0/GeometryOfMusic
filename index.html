Welcome to Geometry of Music

A sandbox for walking through chord space with your hands. The first view embeds ordered two-note chords on a torus.

3D torus

  • Left click a node to pick that dyad.
  • Right drag to rotate.
  • Middle drag to pan.
  • Scroll to zoom.
  • Reset view and Hide torus (top-right) control the camera and the visible geometry; the interval-class legend sits just below them.

Timeline

  • Three tracks, each with its own instrument and curve color.
  • Click a cell to place the current dyad.
  • Click again to clear it.
  • Arrows move the cursor, Enter places, Backspace clears.
  • Drag the right edge of a placed chord to extend or shrink its duration; it snaps to the grid and stops at the next chord.
  • Load familiar progressions from the Preset menu (I–V–vi–IV, Pachelbel, 12-bar blues, ii–V–I).

Transport & theme

  • Play / Stop, BPM, meter, subdivision, bar count.
  • Drag the bar between the 3D view and the timeline to resize them. Double-click to reset.
  • Toggle between dark / light theme at the right of the transport row.
  • Click the ? button in the top bar to see this again.
